NAP Country Tracking Tool

The NAP Process has 4 Elements:
  • A - Laying the ground work and addressing gaps
  • B - Preparatory elements
  • C - Implementation strategies
  • D - Reporting monitoring and review

Each element has certain 'Measures' that show what steps a country has taken towards the NAP process.
